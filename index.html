Who am I?

I am a tenured research scientist (CR) at Université Côte d’Azur, Inria Sophia Antipolis. My research interest is in the development of computational and statistical methods for the joint analysis of biomedical data and brain images, with application to the study of neurodegenerative disorders.

I am also interested in the statistical analysis of clinical trials data, time-series analysis, and in the latest advances in machine learning for the analysis of high dimensional data.
